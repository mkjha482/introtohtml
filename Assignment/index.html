My Childhood

Hello, I am Mayank. I spent most of my childhood in a very small city named Deoghar in India. I completed my basic education there only. I made so many great friends there & enjoyed one of the most amazing period of my life as I learned about life and developed in academics while having no tension. It really is true that when you are just a kid you live the best and most carefree life. I really miss those amazing and absolutely beautiful years of my life.
